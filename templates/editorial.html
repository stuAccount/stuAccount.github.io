Nº 07 About the Author A short biography

Jesse Zen is a Java backend developer based on Earth, building distributed systems and production-grade APIs that hold up under load. The work lives at the intersection of reliability and simplicity — shipping software that survives real traffic, learning by building, and refining the craft one commit at a time.

Most days are spent in the JVM ecosystem: designing service boundaries, tuning garbage collectors, and arguing (politely) about cache invalidation. The rest goes to open source, LeetCode, and the quiet conviction that a well-named variable is its own kind of literature.

— Ship beats perfect. Learn by building.
